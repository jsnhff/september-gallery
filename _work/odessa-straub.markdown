Odessa Straub (b. 1989, Brooklyn, NY) graduated with a BFA from The Cooper Union in 2013. Her solo exhibitions include, Tears in Housebreaking Letting the Gold, Acappella, Napoli, Italy (2016), Necrotizing Woos, Jeffrey Stark, New York, NY (2016), Seasonings on Precipice Perception, Mier Gallery, Los Angeles, CA (2015), and Odessa Straub, NADA, NY. In 2017, Odessa had a two-person exhibition with Agathe Snow at Marlborough Contemporary, Chelsea, NY. Recent group exhibitions include: Blue Jean Baby, SEPTEMBER, Husdon, NY (2016), Fresh Cuts, Eric Firestone, Hamptons, NY (2016); Present Conditional, Mier, Los Angeles, CA (2015); Perfect Present: Three Generations of Painting, Jeffrey Stark, New York, NY (2015); Thatâ€™s The Neighbor, Always Dressing These Boulders In The Yard, The Suzanne Geiss Company, New York, NY (2014). Odessa will be in a forthcoming group exhibition curated by Lauren Taschen at Max Hetzler, Berlin, Germany. Her forthcoming solo exhibition at SEPTEMBER opens July 17, 2017. Odessa Straub lives and works in Brooklyn, NY.
